Weather wearing app
Tic-Tac-Toe is a classic two-player game where opponents take turns marking spaces in a 3x3 grid. The objective of the game is to be the first to complete a row, column, or diagonal with your own symbol, either 'X' or 'O'. Players strategically place their marks to block their opponent from achieving a winning combination while aiming to create their own. With its simple rules and quick gameplay, Tic-Tac-Toe is a timeless game of strategy and anticipation that appeals to players of all ages.
